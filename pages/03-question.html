QUESTION - Choose which of the five words is the odd one out.
CHOICES -
(a) calm
(b) quiet
(c) relaxed
(d) serene
(e) unruffled
ANSWER - (b) quiet
(IN)CORRECT - Right!
EXPLANATION - 'Quiet' is the odd one out, as the others mean the same thing. Although, one being quiet does not always mean that one is calm, relaxed, serene, or unruffled. One could be extremely upset and annoyed but remain quiet.
